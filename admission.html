Courses Offered

We are providing a wide variety of courses covering several domains in both undergraduate and graduate courses are available.

B.Ed

B.Ed or Bachelor of Education is a 2 year professional course that is done after graduation to work as a teacher in schools. 

M.Ed

M.Ed or Master of Education. is a 2 years professional course that prepares educators by nurturing their professional skills 

B.A-B.Ed

B.A B.Ed is an integrated 4 year professional course that is done after graduation to work as a teacher in schools.

B.Sc-B.Ed

B.Sc B.Ed is an integrated 4 year professional course that is done after graduation to work as a teacher in schools.

Shiksha Shastri

A student who has passed either Shastri or B.Ed or equivalent examination with Sanskrit may be admitted to Shiksha Shastri
