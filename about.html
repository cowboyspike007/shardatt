About Us

The A.N. B.Ed College is situated in Ramu Ka Bass, Gokulpura NH-11, Sikar, near Shobha Sariya college, Rajasthan. The campus has been designed and developed to provide optimum environment of 21st Century education where every aspect of teaching and learning is supported by modern and relevant technology.

Teachers play a very important role in a student's life. It is, to a great extent, the teachers who decide the shape of a student's life will take. So, it is very necessary to be adequately equipped with resources that will make the teacher a perfect role model to the students. To achieve this, Master of Education (M. Ed.), Bachelor of Education (B. Ed), BA B.Ed (integrated course), BSc B.Ed (integrated course) and Shiksha Shastri were introduced, which will teach a person about Teaching and the various aspects associated with teaching. Once a person completes the Master of Education (M. Ed) , Bachelor of Education (B. Ed), BA B.Ed, BSc B.Ed and Shiksha Shastri coaching or training, then he is awarded with a M. Ed, B. Ed, BA B.Ed, BSc B.Ed and Shiksha Shastri degree.
