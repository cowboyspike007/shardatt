About Us

Sharda T.T college alone has 20 faculty and almost 15 other support and service staff, who cater for teacher's training programs.

Sharda T.T college was founded 2009 as an advancement towards educating the educators of tomorrow. With the Sharda style of learning; one that inspires students of all disciplines to learn and innovate through practical, hands-on experience. Sharda T.T college has nurtured a legacy of inspiring change-makers, impressive alumni that has impacted change worldwide, faculty members who are at the forefront of their fields, world-class infrastructure, and a cutting-edge digital campus.
